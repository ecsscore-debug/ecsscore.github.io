Our Mission

ECS exists with a single, powerful belief — that every professional in India deserves a verified, portable and trusted career identity. One that isn’t based on claims, assumptions or bias… but on proof.

“To make employment credibility universal, portable and proof‑driven for every professional.”

A mission to bring truth, transparency and trust to India’s hiring ecosystem by creating a standardized, tamper-proof employment identity layer — the ECS Score.

Why This Mission Matters

Because the hiring ecosystem today is broken — fake experience, unverifiable claims, inconsistent references, and biased evaluations make it impossible for employers to trust resumes… and just as impossible for deserving candidates to stand out.

We are fixing this with a verification-first approach, creating a world where careers are built on accuracy, trust and credibility.
